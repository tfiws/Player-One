Cyberpunk 2077

Game Description

Cyberpunk 2077 is a 2020 action RPG developed by CD Projekt Red and published by CD Projekt It takes place in an alternate timeline, based upon the TTRPG series 'Cyberpunk', in the year 2077. You play as 'V', a mercenary looking to make a name for themselves in Night City, one of the most dangerous cities in America

Cyberpunk 2077 allows you to fully customise V, with life altering "Cyberware", which can range from a holographic tattoo, to a complete limb replacement. These modifications allow you to experience a wide range of playstyles, so there's something for everyone.

Platforms

Cyberpunk 2077 was released for PC, Playstation and Xbox

Strategy Guide

Quick Tips

  • Don't generalise, specialise. Find one way you like to play and specialise your perks into it.
  • Don't ignore the side content. Not only can it net you good gear and eddies, it's pretty fun too!
  • Invest in some leg cyberware, the increased mobility is worth the cost.
  • Early game, don't forget about quickhacks
  • Use the environment to your advantage, things like cover, or even explosive barrels can quickly turn the tide in a fight.

Playstyles and Builds

Netrunner

A good netrunner can wipe an entire army, without even entering the building. For this build, you'll want high intelligence, maximum RAM and a strong OS, such as the Militech Canto, or the Tetratronic Rippler MK.5

As a net runner, you should focus on contagious, high damaging hacks that can quickly spread across an entire building

On the rare occasion you should need to draw a weapon, make sure it is a Smart weapon, like the Hercules 3AX or the Yasha.

Suggested build:

  • Body: 20
  • Intelligence: 20
  • Cool: 12
  • Tech: 20
  • Reflexes: 9

Shinobi

A shinobi build focuses on Katanas, throwing weapons, and movement speed.

Cyberwear such as Optical Camo, Sandevistan and the Countershell are all key components of this build to give you the best chance of staying undetected. If you are detected, the countershell will activate, giving you a high mitigation chance.

As a shinobi, you will want to find a strong Katana. A long term goal should be the Byakko, as this is regarded as the strongest katana in the game. A good early-mid game sword is the Errata, with increased fire damage. Alongside your katana, you will want a strong throwing knife. A high levelled standard Knife is a very good option, with a +200% headshot multiplier.

Suggested build:

  • Body: 15
  • Intelligence: 3
  • Cool: 20
  • Tech: 20
  • Reflexes: 20

Commando

A commando will use an assault rifle, which can be used with or without a suppressor. This build doesn't use many fancy tricks or gadgets, it just focuses on pure firepower.

As a commando, you have a wide range of riles to choose from. The overall strongest rifle is the Prejudice, however this requires you to either have finished the game, or to be very close. Another, earlier, choice is the Carmen, as a solid all-rounder.

To maximise your accuracy, you'll want to pick up some strong attachments. Muzzles such as the RC-7 Strigoi increase your critical chance, and reduce recoil.

For cyberware, focus on increasing your damage resistance using subdermal armour, carapaces, and the rangeguard. Kerenzikov + Defenzikov increases your survivability massively, slowing time and improving your mitigation chance.

Suggested build:

  • Body: 18
  • Intelligence: 20
  • Cool: 3
  • Tech: 20
  • Reflexes: 20
