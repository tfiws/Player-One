Cyberpunk 2077

Game Description

Cyberpunk 2077 is a 2020 action RPG developed by CD Projekt Red and published by CD Projekt It takes place in an alternate timeline, based upon the TTRPG series 'Cyberpunk', in the year 2077. You play as 'V', a mercenary looking to make a name for themselves in Night City, one of the most dangerous cities in America

Cyberpunk 2077 allows you to fully customise V, with life altering "Cyberware", which can range from a holographic tattoo, to a complete limb replacement. These modifications allow you to experience a wide range of playstyles, so there's something for everyone.

Platforms

Cyberpunk 2077 was released for PC, Playstation and Xbox
