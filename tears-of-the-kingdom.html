The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom

Game Description

The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom is a 2023 Action Adventure game by Nintendo. You play as Link, as he fights to prevent Ganondorf from destroying Hyrule.

The game is played from a Third-person perspective. It features a large, open world for the player to explore. One interesting feature is the non-linear progression. This means you can choose which order you can complete the story in, even rushing straight to the final boss! Though it may not be advised...

Platforms

The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom was only released for the Nintendo Switch, later being ported to the Switch 2.
