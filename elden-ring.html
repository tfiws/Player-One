Elden Ring

Game Description

Elden Ring is a 2022 Action RPG developed by FromSoftware. It is directed by Hidetaka Miyazaki, and features worldbuilding from George R. R. Martin. You control a customisable character, on a quest to repair the Elden Ring, and become the new Elden Lord.

Elden Ring is played from an over the shoulder, third-person perspective. It features a large, open map, which is split into segment that you, the player, have to find. As is with all FromSoftware games, Elden Ring features incredibly difficult bosses that can take days to beat.

Platforms

Elden Ring was released for PC, Playstation and Xbox

Strategy Guide

Common Mistakes

  • Badly applying points
  • Ignoring stamina
  • Not enough dodging or movement
  • Not upgrading flasks
  • Not exploring, and sticking to the story

Quick Tips:

  • Upgrade Vigor for survivability
  • Explore the map to find Talismans for buffs
  • Find a playstyle that best suits you, and research a build that fits

Playstyles and Builds

Playstyles

Offensive Melee

Due to the range of melee types in Elden Ring, there are many different sub-types of a melee build, however the basics are high strength, vigor and a high enough endurance to carry heavy armour.

Utilise jumping attacks and fully charged heavy attacks with a two handed weapoon for maximum damage.

If you can, use the Bull-Goat armour set, as it provides top tier defense and poise. However, there are also brilliant, lighter, alternatives, such as the Veteran's set, or Radahn's set.

Suggested build:

  • Strength: 60-80
  • Vigor: 50-60
  • Endurance:30+

    • Defensive Melee

    A strong defensive melee build focuses on utilising greatshields to block attacks, and dealing high damage using high poise weapons. You'll want to use either the Great Stars for health regeneration, or a Claymore/Heavy Broadsword for faster guard counters.

    You should aim for the Fingerprint Stone Shield, however the Brass shield is a good alternative early to mid-game. The same armours as the Offensive melee build apply to this one too.

    Talismans such as the Great-Jar's arsenal or Curved sword talisman can be used to boost equipment load or guard counters.

    Suggested build:

  • Vigor: 60
  • Endurance: 40-50
  • Strength: 60-80
  • Faith: 30-40

    • Offensive Magic

    Focusing on high damage output from magic and spells, capable of thousands of damage per hit. Early game, you should use the Meteorite staff, untill you can get Lusat's Glintstone Staff for the best potential damage.

    In your offhand, you should use the Carian Glintstone staff, to boost sword sorceries. Alternatively, you could use Moonveil Katana for a hybrid approach.

    A good armour set to use would be the Azur's Glintstone set

    Suggested build:

  • Intelligence: 80
  • Vigor: 50-60
  • Mind: 30-40
  • Dexterity: 30-40

    • Q&A

    Q:

    How do I get the meteorite staff?

    A:

    The Meteorite Staff is found in the Street of Sages Ruins in the Caelid Swamp of Aeonia, specifically on a corpse hanging out of a small ruined tower window.
